About LSF
Welcome Address

Welcome address

Welcome to the Laboratory of Forest Sciences! We are excited to share our commitment to advancing research and fostering education in the field of biodiversity conservation. As a leading institution, we are dedicated to providing reliable data and credible expertise to support sustainable management practices and contribute to the global efforts in preserving ecosystems for future generations.

At our laboratory, we prioritize collaboration and innovation, working closely with local and international partners to address the most pressing environmental challenges of our time. We believe that scientific research, when paired with community involvement and a sustainable approach, can pave the way for meaningful change.

As the Director of this esteemed laboratory, I invite you to explore the wealth of knowledge we offer and encourage you to join us in our mission. Together, we can create a lasting impact on biodiversity conservation, ensuring that future generations inherit a thriving natural world.

Vision and Mission

Vision

A world in which, Science and Innovation provide relevant and needed knowledge to support sustainable development

Mission

Conduct research and training to provide biodiversity resource managers with reliable data and credible skills to develop, conserve and sustainably manage biodiversity

Core Values
LSFBI is guided by core values which underpin our operations towards achieving our objectives

Excellence

LSFBI will strive for excellence in all its endeavors comprising the conduct and administration of our research and teaching programs.

Integrity

As Director of the LSFBI, I will always do my best to be and remain a living example of integrity in order to show the way of noble behavior to the rest of the members of the Laboratory.

Commitment to partners

The LSFBI partners is composed of scientists, students, staff, decisions makers and any other end users. From data to research products, we are open-mind and committed to satisfy demand driven knowledge to support decision making on biodiversity conservation and sustainable use.

Culture of Mentoring

As leader in forest sciences and biodiversity informatics across Africa we are ready to support and push forwards any initiative in the field of our expertise.

Accountability

We are grateful to our donors at all scale and assured them of a production of data and research products of quality to optimally value their investments.

Shared Governance

The Laboratory comprise scientists, administrators and technicians each of whom provides a perspective to be considered during the process of setting future directions and making decisions.
